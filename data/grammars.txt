Lection1:

Usage of the word です to say "it is...".

Changing the topic with the particle "は".
Example: 山下さんは先生です (Mr. Yamashita is a teacher)

Asking questions by adding か at the end of a statement
Example: 今何時ですか　(What time is it now?)

Using the particle の to combine several nouns.
